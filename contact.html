Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. A standard website typically takes 4-8 weeks, custom software projects can range from 3-9 months, and blockchain implementations may take 6-12 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline specific to your project.

How do you determine project costs?

Our pricing is based on project scope, complexity, timeline, and specific features required. We offer both fixed-price quotes and time-and-materials billing depending on the nature of the project. After understanding your requirements, we'll provide a detailed proposal with transparent pricing.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer various support and maintenance packages to ensure your digital solutions continue to perform optimally. These can include regular updates, security patches, performance monitoring, and technical support. We'll work with you to determine the right level of support for your specific needs.

What technologies do you work with?

We work with a wide range of technologies and platforms, including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (React, Angular, Vue.js), PHP, Python, Node.js, .NET, Java, mobile technologies (iOS, Android, React Native), and blockchain platforms (Ethereum, Hyperledger). We select the most appropriate technology stack based on your specific requirements and business goals.

How do you ensure the security of your solutions?

Security is integrated into every step of our development process. We implement industry-standard security protocols, regular security audits, secure coding practices, data encryption, and compliance with relevant regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.). We also provide security training for clients and conduct penetration testing on completed solutions.
